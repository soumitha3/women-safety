FAQs
  • What steps should I follow to register a workplace safety complaint?

    Navigate to the register complaint button, provide detailed information about the incident, and submit any relevant evidence. This will initiate the complaint resolution process.

  • How does the complaint resolution process work?

    Once a complaint is registered, it is routed to the appropriate authorities or designated personnel for investigation.

  • How can I deal with harassment at work?

    The application offers guidance on addressing workplace harassment, such as documenting incidents, reporting to HR, and seeking support from colleagues.
