Support

  • If you have faced a sexual harassment issue at your workplace, and your complaint to IC is not being acted upon we will attempt to sensitize the employer on the need to take action as per law.
  • If there is no IC in your organization we can help you connect with the Local Committee chaired by the District Collector to prefer your complaint.
  • If you want to exercise your right to recourse under criminal law, we will connect you to the applicable Police Station to prefer your complaint.
